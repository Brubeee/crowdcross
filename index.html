    Before You Cross

    Real Road Safety — 4 Steps Every Pedestrian Must Follow

    👈

    Step 1 — Look Left

    The first threat comes from your left. Check for vehicles approaching from that direction before you even think about stepping off the kerb.

    👉

    Step 2 — Look Right

    Then check your right. On a two-way road, traffic comes from both sides. A car you missed on the right is just as dangerous.

    👈

    Step 3 — Look Left Again

    Left a second time — because in the seconds you spent looking right, the situation on your left has changed. Never assume it's still clear.

    ✅

    Step 4 — Cross Lane by Lane

    Step into one lane at a time. Wait on the median island if needed. Keep looking both ways as you cross — traffic never stops moving.

    Hold SPACE to scan traffic. The meter fills as you look — only then can you step forward.
